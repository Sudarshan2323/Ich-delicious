About Restaurant

Hotel Dazzle is a F&B-Casual Dining outlet located in the heart of the city. It offers a wide variety of delicious dishes from around the world. The restaurant has a cozy and inviting atmosphere, perfect for a casual lunch or dinner. The menu features a variety of appetizers, entrees, and desserts. The staff is friendly and attentive, ensuring that each guest has a pleasant dining experience. The restaurant also offers a selection of wines and beers to complement the meal. Hotel Dazzle is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious meal with friends and family.
